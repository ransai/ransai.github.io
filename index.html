RANSAI 2008

The 6th International Scout Event which is organized by the 1st Kandy Dharmaraja Scout Group and the Old Rajans Scout Association to celebrate the 95th anniversary of scouting at Dharamaraja College and the 30th anniversary of
Old Rajans Scout Association.

1st Kandy Dharmaraja Scout Group

1st Kandy Dharmaraja Scout Group is one of the oldest and the best scout group in Sri Lanka which began in the year 1913.
It has a great history of 95 years in the scout arena in local and international level, while wining
The Kings Flag for three consecutive years from 1917 to 1919 as the scout group with highest number of kings scout in Common Wealth Countries and won the Island Merit Flag from 1993 to 2006 as the best scout group in the island.
1st Kandy Dharmaraja Scout Group and the Old Rajans Scout Association were privileged to organized Five international scout events .
‘Dharmaraja Ccentenary JIM’ to celebrate the 100 years of Dharmaraja College in the year 1987
‘DESAI Jamborate’ International Jamboree to celebrate the 80th anniversary of scouting at Dharmaraja College in the year 1994
‘Rajans Venture’ International adventure
Programme to Celebrate 85th anniversary of scouting at Dharmaraja College in the year 1997
‘Rajans Millennium Jamboree’ in the year 2000
‘Rajans JIM 2003’ International event to celebrate the 90th anniversary of scouting at Dharmaraja College in the year 2003.
